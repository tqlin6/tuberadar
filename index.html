How to read the feed

01

Emerging themes

Topics that many different creators (under 1M subscribers) have started uploading about in the last 12 hours. The clearest signal of a topic catching fire across YouTube — not one big channel posting.

02

Topics gaining momentum

Phrases recurring across the highest-velocity videos on the popular charts right now. Often a format ("tier list"), a topic ("ai agents"), or a hook ("i tried"). Tap to see the videos driving each one.

03

Action

Use the feeds as a brief, not a script. Clone the topic, not the title — the goal is to ride the wave, not to copy what already exists.
