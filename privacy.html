Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This site (TubeRadar) is operated as a free public information resource for content creators. This policy describes what information is collected when you visit and how it is used.

What we collect

TubeRadar itself does not collect, store, or process any personal information. The site is static and does not require an account.

Third-party services

The following third parties may collect information when you visit:

  • Cloudflare Web Analytics. Counts page views and visits in aggregate. Does not use cookies, does not track individual visitors across sites, does not collect IP addresses, and does not fingerprint devices. See the Cloudflare Web Analytics privacy commitment.
  • Google AdSense. Serves advertisements. AdSense and its partners use cookies to measure ad performance and to personalise ads based on your prior visits to this and other sites. You can opt out of personalised advertising at google.com/settings/ads.
  • Google Fonts. Loads typography. Your browser makes a request to fonts.googleapis.com when you visit.
  • GitHub Pages. Hosts the site. GitHub may log visitor IP addresses for security and abuse prevention. See the GitHub Privacy Statement.
  • YouTube. Video thumbnails and links point to youtube.com. Clicking through is governed by Google's privacy policy.

Cookies

TubeRadar does not set its own cookies. Cloudflare Web Analytics does not use cookies either. Third-party services listed above (primarily Google AdSense) may set cookies on your device once ads are enabled. You can disable cookies in your browser settings; this site will continue to function without them, but ads may be less relevant.

Your rights

If you are in the EEA, UK, or California, you have rights under GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA regarding personal information held about you. Because TubeRadar does not collect personal information directly, requests should be directed to the third-party services listed above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to: admin@tuberadar.info.
